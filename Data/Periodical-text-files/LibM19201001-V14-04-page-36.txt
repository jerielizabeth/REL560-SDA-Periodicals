O THOMAS JEFFERSON Author of the Declaration of Independence I have sworn upon the altar of Cod eternal hostility against every form of tyranny over the mind of man.Ñ Writings of Thomas Jefferson, Ford Edition, Vol. VII, page 460. I am for freedom of the press, and against all violations of the Constitution to silence by force and not by reason the complaints or criticisms, just or unjust, of our citizens against their agents. Ñ Id., page 328. Our liberty depends on the freedom of the press, and that can not be limited without being lost.Ñ Id., Vol. IV, page 132. Where the press is free and every man able to read, all is safe.Ñ Id., Vol. X, page 4.
