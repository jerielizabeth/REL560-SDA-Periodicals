FOREFATHERS' ,MONUMENT, PLYMOUTH, MASS. On the front of this monument is this inscription: "National Monument to the Forefathers, erected by a grate-ftil people in remembrance of their labors, sacrifices, and suffer-ings for the cause of Civil and Religious.Liberty." This is a case in which men builded better than they knew. They sought liberty only for themselves; others went beyond them. and asserted liberty for all men.
